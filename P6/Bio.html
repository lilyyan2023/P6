About Me

My name is Mingze Yan. You can call me lily. I am a current sophomore at Northwestern University, majoring CS and minoring Data Science and Economics.

I am from Panjin, China. A small city in northeastern part of China.

My career interests are Software Engineer, analyst, and product manager. My CS interested areas are AI/ML, Computer Vision, Human-Computer Interaction, and full-stack development.

My interests are swimming, jogging, reading books, watching suspense drama, and dancing.

2012

When I was 12 years old, I went to a middle school in Shenyang, a larger city in our province. In the middle school, I learnt many advanced math and science that allowed me to excel in these fields.

2015

After deciding to pursue more opportunities in US, I applied to a US high school called Worcester Academy. It was a wonderful start. I started exploring my way to athletics, science, and math. I joined JV team in soccer and swimming. I also founded the first science-focused club on campus.

2016

Continuing my exploration in high school, I pivoted to swimming and thanks to my hard work during the summer, I advanced to varsity team in swimming. I challenged myself a lot in academics this year, which made me realize how to study more efficiently. Sophomore year was definitely a transition in my high school. I also joined our school math team to compete in regional math competitions representing our school.

2017

Junior year was definitely the busiest. I continued to challenge myself academically. It was also this year, I became our school's math team captain and worked on engaging more members to participate in math contests. Our team were able to advance to statewide competitions that year and I also represented our school to participate in Harvard and MIT math tournaments.

2018

2019

Freshman year, I was super excited to become a college student. I met many interesting and talented wildcats. I got to join .dev, EPIC software team, NU solar car team, Society Of Women Engineer and Spoon. I also took many CS and Econ classes to teach me many professional knowledge.
