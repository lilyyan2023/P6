Flood Depth Estimation
In 2020 summer, I interned in Argonne National Laboratory where I worked in the subteam called Sage where we used Artificial Intelligence(AI) and Computer Vision(CV) for climate research. My research topic specifically was to develop an AI and CV algorithm to estimate flood depth from images. I applied python web scraping to collect over 700 images from social media such as Twitter and Flickr. I also used Python and PyTorch to implement the face-recognition and semantic segmentation algorithm to estimate the flood depth. During the process, there definitely were some challenges and obstacles, but I overcame them with the support of my peers and research mentors. This experience was truly rewarding and I would use the skills I have learnt to explore more future AI and CV related projects.
